Blue Forest Hike Club

Relax in Nature. Work out. Build a team.

Come join our community of naturelovers whose focus is having fun and spending quality time in Nature!

Blue Forest Hike Club is a free hiking group sponsored by UJVVAR.

"I meet these guys every weekend. We discover beautiful places every time and learn a lot about our local flora and fauna."

-Jack B. - musician, philosopher, hike-addict

Join us!

We meet every Saturday morning at 9 AM at the Blue Forest park.
